## Judging Criteria

### 1. Stickiness & Product Market Fit (25%)
- Does it resonate with Seeker community?
- Does it create habits and drive daily engagement?
- **Our angle:** 
  - **One free question every day** — creates daily habit
  - Lucky numbers — daily lottery insight
  - Daily horoscope — morning ritual
  - Push notifications for daily luck

### 2. User Experience (25%)
- Intuitive, polished, easy to navigate
- Enjoyable to use
- **Our focus:** 
  - **Pixel art RPG dialogue style** — NPCs guide users through readings
  - Ancient sage meets retro game — 3,000 years of wisdom, 16-bit aesthetic
  - Interactive quest-like experience — not boring static readings
  - Clean mobile-first, fast readings

### 3. Innovation / X-Factor (25%)
- Novel and creative idea
- Stand out from rest
- **Our X-Factor: 3,000 years of Chinese metaphysical wisdom**
  - **八字 (Four Pillars)** — Complete birth chart analysis
  - **紫微斗數 (Purple Star)** — Destiny palace readings  
  - **五行 (Five Elements)** — Balance & recommendations
  - **生肖 (Zodiac)** — Compatibility & personality
  - **Lucky numbers** — Daily lottery numbers
  - **Love compatibility** — Relationship analysis
  - **Finance forecast** — Wealth & career predictions
  - **Decision guidance** — Unsure about big choices? Get clarity
  - **Life clarity** — Confusion about career, relationships, next steps?
  - **Daily readings** — Start your day with insight
  - All on mobile + SOL payments

### 4. Presentation & Demo Quality (25%)
- Clear communication
- Demo showcases core concept
- Key features + unique selling points
- **Our demo:** Live reading, lucky numbers, payment flow
